You can try experimenting with a habit for 4 weeks (28 days).

  • Print out this paper (these instructions won't show up on your printout)
  • Write down the habit to the right of each 4 week block.
  • Color in a day to track whether you completed it or not

These could be actual experiments that you want to try and not commit to, or they could be habits that you intend to turn into a long-term thing. Either way, doing it for 4 weeks is a lot less intimidating than committing to it for a whole year.

After 13 of these experiments you would have completed a whole year with a lot more information about yourself. Good luck!
